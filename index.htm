Developed marketing and ecommerce production tools to deploy dynamic web content, do A/B testing, and track success.

As a product designer, I met with users for insight into business needs and workflow to develop product vision, user personas, and journeymaps. I collaborated with other designers, product teams, and developers to bring ideas from sketches to final implementation. I produced clickable prototypes for early design testing and conducted beta testing to iterate and improve the product.

IRIS+ was a ground up rebuild of the "in house" marketing production tool to deploy and track campaigns.

Production teams would enter their HTML, js, css, and email content into the system then marketers would schedule the content for deployment, conduct A/B tests, and track performance.

Funnelcake was an ecommerce tool for taking orders and providing the customer further buying options. IRIS+ and Funnelcake shared many common tasks and user base. The long term goal was to unify the experience of both products making the transition from one to the other seemless.
